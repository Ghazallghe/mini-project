                                                                 





                                                             In this game you gotta guess a word to save your man from hanging :)


                                                     1.If your word has less than 9 alphabets each wrong answer will complete your hangman.

                                                     2.If your word has more than 9 alphabets every 2 wrong answers will complete your hangman.

                                                     3.Each time you win you will earn 5 scores.

                                                     4.You have HINT in this game which reveals one alphabet but you can use it once in the game and it will reduces 10 scores.

                                                     5.In leaderboard you can see your place between other players.
                                                     
                                                     * all of the words contain small alphabets.


                                                                                 Wish you enjoy the game :)
